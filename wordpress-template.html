Taxi Pickup Point at Kochi Airport

Cochin International Airport (CIAL) is one of India's busiest international gateways. The taxi pickup point is located just outside the arrival terminal. With Tour with Anand, you don't have to figure it out alone – our drivers will guide you to the right location.

Domestic Arrivals: Meet at the designated taxi zone outside Terminal 1.

International Arrivals: Driver will wait outside Terminal 3 with signage if needed.

Clear communication: You'll receive a call/WhatsApp from the driver as soon as your flight lands.
